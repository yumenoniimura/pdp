Sugar's Story

This is Sugar (HWTH#16343A). She is a Jack Russell Terrier mix, DOB: 9/9/08. Sugar weighs approximately 14 pounds and stands 13 inches at the shoulders. Her owner passed away. Unfortunately, Sugar does not get along well with the family's cat so the family has relinquished Sugar to Halfway to Home to find her another loving Forever home. This is a very loving, healthy little girl who enjoys belly rubs, loves to play with balls, likes to cuddle, will probably want to be your lap dog and bed buddy if you so choose and also has a high energy side to her personality. She walks well on leash and will need to have regular daily exercise to keep her fit and occupied. Sugar is smart and responds to some obedience hand signals and is coming along well with being crate trained. She does need some house training and is a "submissive" pee-er (she pees when excited or when strongly corrected). Sugar is good with small dogs but may need to be introduced slowly because she is a little rambunctious with them at first, it is unknown how she is with large dogs. Sugar will need lots of love, structure and good-quality human companionship to help her bond with her new owner. For more information on Sugar, please contact Suzanne at tele # 661-824-4495.
